Tiger

Tigers can reach a length of up to 3.3 metres (11 feet)

  • Scientific name: Panthera tigris
  • Lifespan: 20 – 26 years (In captivity)
  • Mass: 140 – 670 lbs (Adult)
  • Diet: Carnivore

Tigers are the biggest species of the cat family. They are good swimmers and can swim up to 6 kilometres and have been known to reach speeds up to 40 mph. Tigers can easily jump over 5 metres in length.
